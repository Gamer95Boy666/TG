Dante is the recurring protagonist of the Devil May Cry franchise, and the main playable character for the majority of the games in the series.
He is the second son of the demon Sparda and the human Eva, and the younger twin brother of Vergil.


Devil May Cry is an action adventure hack and slash video game series developed and released by Capcom and created by Hideki Kamiya. The series is about the main character Dante's goals to avenge his mother's murder by exterminating demons.
